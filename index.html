Our Story

It all started with a simple text...
That read “Hi! I’m Mira, how are you?” Dazed and confused looking at this unexpected text I replied “ Mira from New Brunswich???” As I waited for her response that felt like a lifetime, I could picture Mira rolling her eyes back and shrieking “Ah… yeah?!?!.” A whole 10 minutes later she responded “yes”. That simple text turned into a stream of texts and eventually the next day we had what I like to call it "the conversation of a lifetime." We both connected instantaneously and our first conversation lasted for over 3 hours. Soon, we spoke everyday, sometimes multiple times a day for hours at a time. We spoke about everything, from hobbies and sports to which movies scared us the most. Our conversations became second nature, as if we had known each other for our entire lives and before we knew we were inseparable."
